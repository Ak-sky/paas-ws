Powerful Features

Our AI-powered drones provide unmatched capabilities for modern agriculture

Advanced Crop Analysis

Our AI algorithms analyze multispectral imagery to detect crop health issues weeks before they're visible to the human eye, allowing for early intervention.

Precision Application

Target specific areas with precise application of water, fertilizers, and pesticides, reducing waste and environmental impact while maximizing efficiency.

Real-time Insights

Access actionable insights through our intuitive dashboard, with real-time data processing that helps you make informed decisions immediately.

Autonomous Operation

Our drones operate autonomously with pre-programmed flight paths, automated takeoff and landing, and obstacle avoidance systems for hassle-free monitoring.

Weather Resilient

Designed to operate in diverse weather conditions with wind resistance up to 25 mph and IP54 weather sealing to ensure consistent field monitoring.

Seamless Integration

Easily integrate with existing farm management systems and export data in standard formats for compatibility with your current agricultural software.

How It Works

Implementing CocoBot technology is simple and effective

1

Deploy

Set up your CocoBot drone with our easy-to-follow configuration process and define your field boundaries.

2

Scan

The drone autonomously scans your fields on a schedule you determine, collecting multispectral data.

3

Analyze

Our AI algorithms process the collected data to identify issues and opportunities in your crops.

4

Act

Receive actionable insights through our platform and implement targeted interventions to optimize crop health.
